Selected Interface:
The “Main Roote” drink machine in RISD's Met dining hall
Questions:
Sketch & Description:

There are two nearly identical drink machines in the Met that stand on opposite sides of the "drink" corner of the Met. The corner has a stack of clean, empty cups on the left side.
The Main Roote drink machines dispense cubed ice and a variety of soft drinks such as cola, diet cola, ginger ale, and lemonade. The dispensers work by pressing a cup against a vertical, plastic bar which will dispense the corresponding substance for the duration that the bar is pushed. The ice is dispensed in a similar way but with a wider, square bar to press into.
